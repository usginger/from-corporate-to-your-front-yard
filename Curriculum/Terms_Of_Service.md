# 📜 Terms of Service & Behavioral Agreement
### Operational Policies, Student Expectations, & Program Compliance

Welcome to our client services portal. To maintain the elite quality, structural momentum, and premium focus of our **"Handwriting-First" Blueprint**, all enrolled families and corporate clients are required to review and adhere to the operational policies outlined below. 

By initiating enrollment, booking an active training block, or participating in our 15-minute intake assessment, you agree to these professional terms of service.

---

## 🗂️ 1. Financial Investment & Program Packages

* **Tuition Structure:** To ensure consistent milestone execution, coaching services are booked as comprehensive training blocks rather than drop-in sessions. 
* **Youth Accelerator Block:** Fixed as a structured **9-week blueprint** consisting of weekly 2-hour intensive sessions. Tuition is due in full prior to the first session unless a structured installment plan has been explicitly authorized in writing.
* **Corporate Executive Block:** Booked and tracked in dedicated **10-hour strategy blocks**. 
* **Remittance:** Payments are collected securely and professionally via Zelle, Venmo, or certified check immediately upon invoice generation.

---

## 📅 2. Rigid Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

Because we strictly cap our active client slots to maintain an intensive, personalized focus, scheduling slots are highly competitive. 

* **The 24-Hour Rule:** Clients must provide a minimum of **24 hours’ advance notice** via text or phone call for any cancellation or rescheduling requests.
* **Late Cancellations & No-Shows:** Cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice, as well as no-shows, will be charged the **full tuition rate** for that scheduled session. No exceptions will be granted.
* **Emergency Allowance:** We recognize that acute medical or family emergencies happen. A single, one-time emergency waiver may be extended per 9-week training block at the sole discretion of the coach.
* **Coach-Initiated Rescheduling:** If the coach must reschedule due to unforeseen travel or logistical conflicts, a makeup session will be prioritized within 7 business days at a mutually agreeable time.

---

## ⏱️ 3. Punctuality & Session Mechanics

* **Arrival Protocol:** For in-home coaching ("front yard" sessions), please ensure the student's workspace is clear, clean, and set up 5 minutes prior to the scheduled start time. For online corporate sessions, please log into the secure digital portal 2 minutes early to test audio/video connections.
* **Late Arrivals:** Sessions begin exactly at the scheduled time. If a student is late, the session will still conclude at its original scheduled end time to protect subsequent clients' appointments. Lost time due to student tardiness will not be refunded or extended.

---

## 📈 4. Student Behavioral Expectations

Our methodology replaces passive digital entertainment with intense cognitive focus, muscle discipline, and active recall. To achieve the 9-week acceleration milestone, students must adhere to the following standards:

* **The Zero-Device Rule:** All cellular phones, tablets, smartwatches, and video game systems must be muted and placed entirely out of sight inside the workspace. Screens are completely banned unless explicitly required as a diagnostic tool by the coach.
* **Active Engagement:** Students are expected to approach each 2-hour block with an open mind, a respectful attitude, and a willingness to try physical writing exercises. 
* **The Chore-Free Mindset:** We blend intense handwriting discipline with high-value positive reinforcement (incentive-based rewards like our signature White Rabbit Candy ecosystem). Students are expected to celebrate their progress and participate fully in the gamified milestones.

---

## 🤝 5. Parental & Stakeholder Support (For Youth Tracks)

To sustain the momentum of our curriculum between weekly visits, parents are expected to maintain active compliance with our **At-Home Handwriting Guide**:
* Provide a quiet, well-lit, distraction-free home workspace.
* Gently monitor muscle discipline (tripod pencil grip and posture rules) during short home practice intervals.
* Never execute the child's writing assignments or allow them to copy characters backward using predictive screen shortcuts.
